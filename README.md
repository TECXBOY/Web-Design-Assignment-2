# SaloneBlood – Open-Source Blood Registry Network

A digital public good connecting patients in need with voluntary local blood donors across Sierra Leone. Real-time, SMS-based, no internet required.

## Overview

**SaloneBlood** is a web-based emergency blood registry system designed for Sierra Leone's healthcare infrastructure. It eliminates frantic WhatsApp posts and disconnected donor networks by providing:

- **Real-time critical blood shortage tracking** by district
- **Instant SMS alerts** to registered donors (no internet required)
- **One-click registration** for both donors and emergency requesters
- **Live system** covering 8 districts with 347+ registered donors

## Key Features

### For Donors
- Register with blood type, district, and phone number (60 seconds)
- Receive SMS alerts when your blood type is needed nearby
- Walk into clinic—no complex procedures, just save a life

### For Hospitals & Patients
- Post urgent blood requests in real-time
- View live shortage data filtered by district
- Receive donor responses via SMS coordination

## How It Works

### Three-Step Process

1. **Register Your Type** – Create a free donor profile with blood type, district, and phone number
2. **Receive SMS Alert** – Instant SMS when a nearby hospital needs your blood type
3. **Walk Into Clinic** – Show up at the listed facility and save a life

## Usage

1. **As a Donor:**
   - Scroll to "Register as a Donor"
   - Fill in: Full Name, Phone Number, Blood Type, District
   - Click "Register & Enable SMS Alerts"
   - Receive SMS when blood is needed

2. **For Blood Requests:**
   - Click "⚡ EMERGENCY REQUEST"
   - Or view critical shortages in the "Urgent Shortages" section
   - Contact matching donors via SMS
